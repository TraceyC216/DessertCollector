About the Dessert Collector

A place to collect your favorite dessert recipes. Use the convenient calendar feature to properly organize the steps leading up to baking day.

Once you know your guest list, use the allergens guide so you can be confident all of your guests can enjoy your special treats.


How to use the Dessert Collector
  • To search for dessert ideas, explore the View All My Desserts page
  • If you select one of the suggested options, select it and schedule your prep dates using the built in calendar
  • Do you see a favorite dessert missing from the list? Feel free to include it on the Add a Dessert page.
